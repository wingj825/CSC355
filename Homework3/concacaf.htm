CONCACAF consists of Countries that are located in either of the following:

  • North America
  • Central America
  • The Carribean

    • As of 2015, no country from CONCACAF has ever won the FIFA World Cup, The most recent Host of the FIFA World Cup in CONCACAF is the United States of America in 1994.
