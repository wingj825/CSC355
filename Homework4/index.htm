CONCACAF : CONCACAF stands for "Confederation of North, Central American and Caribbean Association Football". Teams in this federation consist of countries from North America, Central America, and the Carribean. In 2018, CONCACAF will have 3-4 countries qualify for the World Cup in Russia.

CONMEBOL : CONCEMBOL stands for "Confederación Sudamericana de Fútbol". Teams in this confederation consist of countries in South America. In 2018, CONMEBOL will have 4-5 countries qualify for the World Cup in Russia.

UEFA : UEFA stands for "Union of European Football Associations". Teams in this confederation consist of countries in Europe. In 2018, UEFA will have 14 countries qualify for the World Cup in Russia. Since Russia is hosting the World Cup, they are the first of the 14 UEFA teams to qualify for the world cup.

CAF : CAF Stands for "Confederation of African Football". Teams in this confederation consist of countries in Africa. In 2018, CAF will have 5 countries qualify for the World Cup in Russia.

AFC : AFC Stands for "Asian Football Confederation". Teams in this confederation consist of countries in Asia, Australia, and the Middle East. In 2018, AFC will have 4-5 countries qualify for the World Cup in Russia.

OFC : OFC Stands for "Oceania Football Confederation". Teams in this confederation consist of countries in Oceania and the Pacific Islands. In 2018, CAF will have 0-1 countries qualify for the World Cup in Russia.
